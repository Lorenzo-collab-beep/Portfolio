About me

Let me tell you something about my story.

Hello, my name is Lorenzo,

I am a software developer with professional experience in the electronic payments, where I have worked both as a consultant and as an employee. My main focus has been on the C language, which—along with its successor C++—remains one of my favorites for the freedom it offers when working close to the hardware. For more details about my education and professional background, feel free to visit my LinkedIn profile.

In my free time, I keep exploring other areas of programming and experimenting with modern languages, especially Python for scripting, artificial intelligence, and web application development.

Beyond programming, I am passionate about science (especially space exploration and astrophysics), outdoor activities, good cuisine, motorcycles (I ride a KTM Duke 790), and recently the game of Padel (I'm proud of my Head Bolt Pro racket).

I created this portfolio to highlight some of my personal projects and to provide an overview of my skills. This website itself was developed with the Python Flask framework and then converted into a static site using Flask-Frozen, for easier deployment on GitHub Pages.

For all of that and more, have a look at my GitHub profile.
